Multiple projects involving firmware-level modification of graphics cards. These include cross-flashing an Nvidia Quadro FX card to run in a PowerMac G5 Linux system, and various other firmware modifications to enable non-standard hardware configurations or unlock disabled hardware features. These projects require understanding of GPU firmware structures, PCI device identification, and driver compatibility at the firmware level.
