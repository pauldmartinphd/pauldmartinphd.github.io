Multiple projects involving physical modification of CPU pins to enable non-standard processor configurations in consumer hardware. Examples include mounting server-class Xeon processors in desktop LGA 775 motherboards through pin modification, overclocking Pentium Wolfdale processors via pin mod, and similar hardware-level modifications to Dell Precision and other platforms. These projects require understanding of the electrical signaling between the CPU and motherboard at the pin level.
