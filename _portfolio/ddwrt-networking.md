Multiple projects involving replacement of factory firmware on Linksys WRT54G routers with open-source alternatives (dd-wrt, Tomato) to enable advanced networking capabilities including wireless repeating, traffic filtering, and custom firewall rules. These projects involve firmware flashing, network protocol configuration, and understanding of embedded Linux systems on MIPS-based router hardware.
